The Bachelor of Public Health (BPH) program envisions becoming a leading undergraduate program in public health, recognized for its excellence in education, research, and community engagement. We aim to develop future public health leaders who are equipped to address global health challenges and promote health equity. Mission Statement Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and interdisciplinary education in public health that prepares students to protect and improve the health of populations. We are committed to: Academic Excellence: Offering a rigorous curriculum that covers the core areas of public health, including epidemiology, biostatistics, health policy, environmental health, and social and behavioral sciences. Innovative Research: Encouraging undergraduate research opportunities that contribute to the advancement of public health knowledge and practices.

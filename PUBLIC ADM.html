In the Faculty of public administration, you will study for a duration of 4 years, during which we will provide you with lessons from specialized instructors in the field. The cost is $150 for online courses. You also have the option to take courses offline.

To determine how many departments typically exist within a Bachelor's program in Public Administration, it's useful to consider the common academic structure of such programs. Generally, a Bachelor of Public Administration (BPA) program will have several departments or specialized areas of focus, including but not limited to: Public Policy and Administration: Covers the fundamentals of public administration and policy-making processes. Public Finance: Focuses on budgeting, financial management, and economic principles within the public sector. Human Resources Management: Studies personnel management, labor relations, and organizational behavior in public organizations. Urban Planning and Development: Deals with the planning, development, and management of urban areas.

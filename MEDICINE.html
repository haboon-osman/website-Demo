In the Faculty of medicine, you will study for a duration of 4 years, during which we will provide you with lessons from specialized instructors in the field. The cost is $200 for online courses. You also have the option to take courses offline.

The Bachelor of Medicine program typically encompasses a wide range of departments that provide the necessary education and training for future medical professionals. While the specific structure can vary between universities, a standard Bachelor of Medicine program might include the following departments:

  • Physiology
  • Biochemistry
  • Pharmacology
  • Pathology
  • Microbiology
  • Immunology
  • Genetics
  • Public Health
  • Medical Ethics and Law
  • Clinical Skills
  • Internal Medicine

Computer Science Focuses on algorithms, programming languages, software development, and theoretical foundations of computing. Information Systems Concentrates on the design, implementation, and management of information systems in organizational contexts. Network and Security Deals with network architecture, security protocols, cybersecurity, and risk management. Software Engineering Emphasizes software design, development, testing, and maintenance. Data Science and Analytics Covers data management, data mining, machine learning, and statistical analysis. Human-Computer Interaction Focuses on the design and evaluation of user interfaces and the study of human-computer interaction. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Involves the study of AI algorithms, neural networks, and machine learning applications. Web Development and Multimedia Centers on web technologies, multimedia content development, and internet applications. IT Project Management Teaches project management principles, agile methodologies, and IT project lifecycle management. Emerging Technologies Covers topics related to the latest technological advancements such as blockchain, IoT, and cloud computing.
